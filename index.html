Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is Havan Samidha?

Havan Samidha refers to the small pieces of wood that are used as the primary offering in a Havan or fire ritual. These wood pieces are typically from specific sacred trees like mango, banyan, or pipal and are carefully selected for their auspicious properties and ability to sustain the sacred fire.

What are the scientific benefits of Havan?

Havan has several scientific benefits. It purifies the air by releasing formic aldehyde which kills harmful bacteria. The smoke from Havan reduces fungal load in enclosed spaces and has been found effective against fungi like Aspergillus and Penicillium. Additionally, the process creates alpha brain waves that promote mental clarity and relaxation. For more detailed information, visit our Scientific Benefits section.

Yes, all our products are quality certified to ensure that you receive the highest standard of puja and havan materials. We adhere to strict quality control measures throughout our manufacturing process to maintain the purity and authenticity that our customers expect from traditional ritual products.

What is the shelf life of your products?

The shelf life varies depending on the product. Dry products like Havan Samidha and Moli can last for 12-18 months when stored properly in cool, dry conditions. Liquid products such as Gulab Jal and Ganga Jal have a shelf life of 6-12 months. Each product package includes specific storage instructions and best-before dates.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products internationally. We have experience in exporting Puja and Havan products to various countries while complying with international shipping regulations. Additional shipping charges and delivery time will vary based on the destination country. Please contact us for specific information about international shipping options.

Our products stand out because we prioritize authenticity and purity. We source our materials ethically, maintain traditional preparation methods, and avoid chemical additives. Our manufacturing process combines ancient wisdom with modern quality control to ensure that each product embodies the true essence of Vedic traditions while meeting contemporary standards for safety and efficacy.

What is the correct way to perform Havan?

The correct way to perform Havan involves several steps including setup of the fire altar (havan kund), purification rituals, kindling the sacred fire, recitation of mantras, and making offerings of ghee, herbs, and samidha. The specific procedure can vary based on the purpose and tradition. We provide detailed guidelines with our Havan kits, and our team is available to answer specific questions about ritual procedures.
